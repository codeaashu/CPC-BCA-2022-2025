How It Works

1

Login or Sign Up

Sign up using your email if you're new or log in if you're already a user. Simple and secure.

2

Access Dashboard

Once logged in, you’ll land on your personal home page where you can track expenses or create new groups.

3

Create a Group

Going on a trip? Create a group with your friends, add 4 members (or more), and invite them to join.

4

Add Expenses

Each member can log their expenses, whether it's food, travel, stay, or other shared costs.

5

Smart Calculation

SPLITIT automatically calculates who owes whom — giving you a simple, clean summary of who pays what to whom.
